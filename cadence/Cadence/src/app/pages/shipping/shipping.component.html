Returns Policy

Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer and are non-refundable. For high-value items (over 75 DT), we recommend using a trackable shipping service or purchasing insurance.

Gift Returns

Items marked as a gift will receive a gift credit for the value of the return. If the item was not marked as a gift, the refund will be issued to the original purchaser.
